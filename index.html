Command Reference

Just type a command to get started. The skill handles the rest.

/start Full onboarding — identifies your stage and priorities
/help See all available commands
/focus Lock into one task until it's done
/sprint 60-minute deep work with 3-5 micro-tasks
/recover Get unstuck with the smallest next step
/decide Compare options and commit to one
/energy Route tasks based on your current energy
/binder Build your 17-section investor binder
/score Rate your investor binder readiness
/pitch Draft your pitch (verbal, deck, or one-sheet)
/deck Build or review your pitch deck
/metrics Review your key startup metrics
/runway Calculate burn rate and runway
/outreach Draft sales or investor outreach messages
/validate Run customer validation exercises

Get Started in 3 Steps

Install the skill, answer three questions, and start building.

1

Install

Download from GitHub Releases and upload to Claude.ai Settings, or clone the repo.

2

Build
