# Investor Templates

Load this file when building investor-facing documents: executive summary, cold outreach, monthly updates, LOIs, pitch intros.

---

## Executive Summary (1-Page)

```
[COMPANY NAME]
[Tagline — one sentence that says what you do]

PROBLEM
[2–3 sentences: who has it, how painful, how big]

SOLUTION
[2–3 sentences: what you do, how it works, key differentiator]

TRACTION
[Best metric + growth signal — e.g., "$12K MRR, growing 20% MoM"]

MARKET
TAM: $XB (source) | Beachhead: $XM — [specific segment]

BUSINESS MODEL
[How you make money — 1–2 sentences]

TEAM
[Founder 1]: [Relevant credential or why they're the right person]
[Founder 2]: [Relevant credential]

THE ASK
Raising: $[X] on a [SAFE / Seed round]
Use of funds: [Product X%] / [GTM X%] / [Team X%]
Milestone: [Specific goal — e.g., $50K MRR, Series A readiness]

[Name] | [Email] | [Website] | [Calendly link]
```

## Cold Investor Outreach Email

```
Subject: [Company] — [One-line value prop]

Hi [Investor Name],

[1 sentence — why them specifically. Reference their portfolio or stated thesis.]

[Company] helps [ICP] [specific outcome] — [traction signal in one sentence].

We're raising $[X] on a [SAFE / priced round]. Would love 20 minutes if this fits your thesis.

[Your name]
[Email] | [Website] | [Deck link]
```

## Warm Intro Request (to send to a mutual connection)

```
Hi [Name],

I'm looking to connect with [Investor Name] at [Firm]. Would you be willing to make an intro?

Here's a blurb you can forward:

"[Founder Name] is building [Company] — [one sentence: what they do and for whom].
They have [traction signal]. They're raising [amount] and I thought you might want to meet.
[Founder email / deck link]"

Happy to make it easy — just let me know and I'll send you anything you need.

Thanks,
[Your name]
```

## Monthly Investor Update

```
Subject: [Company] — [Month Year] Update

Hi [Investor(s)],

Here's our [Month] update:

HIGHLIGHTS
• [Win 1 — specific and positive]
• [Win 2 — specific and positive]

METRICS
• MRR: $X (↑/↓ X% MoM)
• Customers: X (↑ X new, ↓ X churned)
• Runway: X months

CHALLENGES
• [Honest challenge — investors respect founders who name their problems]

ASK
• [Specific request: intro to X, feedback on Y, connection to Z]

Thank you,
[Name]
```

**Rule:** Send even when things are hard. Silence breeds anxiety. Transparency builds trust.

## Post-Meeting Follow-Up

```
Subject: Re: [Company] — Thank you + next steps

Hi [Name],

Thank you for the time today — really helpful conversation.

A few follow-ups on what we discussed:
• [Point 1 they raised — addressed]
• [Point 2 they raised — addressed]

As mentioned, I'm attaching [exec summary / deck / model].

[Clear next step: "Happy to schedule a follow-up call next week" or "I'll send the data room by Friday."]

[Your name]
```

## Investor Update After a Bad Month

```
Subject: [Company] — [Month] Update (honest one)

Hi [Investor(s)],

[Month] was harder than we expected. Here's the honest picture:

WHAT HAPPENED
• [Specific thing that didn't go as planned]
• [Revenue/metric] came in at $X vs. our target of $X

WHAT WE LEARNED
• [Root cause — specific, not defensive]

WHAT WE'RE DOING ABOUT IT
• [Action 1 already underway]
• [Action 2 starting this week]

METRICS
• MRR: $X | Runway: X months

ASK
• [Specific ask if needed — intro to someone who can help, feedback, advice]

[Name]
```
