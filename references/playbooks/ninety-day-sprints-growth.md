# 90-Day Sprint: Stage 3 — Growth to Scale-Ready

> **Prerequisite:** Complete Stage 0–2 sprints in `ninety-day-sprints.md` first.

---

## Stage 3: Growth — 90 Days to Scale-Ready

**Goal:** Go from "repeatable sales" to "systems, team, and metrics that are ready for investment or aggressive growth."

### Weeks 1-2: Systems Audit

**Deliverables:**
- Audit of all manual processes (list everything you do by hand)
- Top 5 processes to automate or systematize
- Tool stack finalized (CRM, billing, support, analytics)
- SOPs written for top 3 recurring tasks

---

### Weeks 3-4: Team Buildout Plan

**Deliverables:**
- Org chart for next 12 months (who you need, when)
- Job descriptions for next 2 hires
- Compensation research for each role
- Recruiting pipeline started (job posts live, outreach begun)

---

### Weeks 5-6: Unit Economics Lock-In

**Deliverables:**
- Fully calculated unit economics: CAC, LTV, payback period, gross margin
- Break-even analysis: revenue needed to cover all costs
- Scenario model: what happens at 2x, 5x, 10x current customers?
- Pricing adjusted if unit economics don't work

**Unit economics template:**
```
Revenue per customer/month:     $[___]
Cost to acquire (CAC):          $[___]
Cost to serve/month:            $[___]
Gross margin per customer:      $[___]
Months to payback CAC:          [___]
Expected customer lifespan:     [___] months
Lifetime value (LTV):           $[___]
LTV:CAC ratio:                  [___]:1    (target: 3:1+)
```

---

### Weeks 7-8: Fundraising Prep (if applicable)

**Deliverables:**
- Investor one-pager updated with real metrics
- 10-slide pitch deck drafted
- Financial model: 12-month projection with assumptions
- Target investor list: 30 names with warm intro paths

**Skip this if bootstrapping.** Instead, use Weeks 7-8 for:
- Profitability plan: path to covering all costs from revenue
- Reinvestment plan: how profits get deployed for growth
- Cash reserve target: 3-6 months of operating expenses

---

### Weeks 9-10: Growth Experiments

**Deliverables:**
- 3 growth experiments designed and launched
- Each experiment has: hypothesis, metric, timeline, success criteria
- Budget allocated per experiment
- Weekly check-in on experiment progress

**Experiment format:**
```
Hypothesis: If we [action], then [metric] will [change] by [amount].
Metric: [specific number we're measuring]
Timeline: [2-4 weeks]
Budget: $[amount]
Success criteria: [specific threshold]
Failure criteria: [when to kill it]
```

---

### Weeks 11-12: Scale-Ready Assessment

**Deliverables:**
- Scale-readiness scorecard completed
- Board or advisor update sent
- Next 90-day plan drafted (growth, fundraise, or optimize)
- Stage 3 retrospective

**Scale-readiness scorecard:**

| Area | Ready | Not Ready |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Product-market fit | NPS 40+, low churn, inbound demand | High churn, constant pivoting |
| Repeatable sales | Defined process, non-founder closes | Founder does everything |
| Unit economics | LTV:CAC > 3:1, payback < 12 months | Losing money per customer |
| Team | Key roles filled, culture documented | Solo founder doing everything |
| Systems | Core ops automated, metrics tracked | Manual everything, no dashboards |
| Cash | 6+ months runway or profitable | <3 months, no plan |

**4+ Ready = scale. 3+ Not Ready = optimize before growing.**

## Weekly Checkpoint Template

Use this every Friday. Takes 15 minutes.

```
WEEKLY CHECKPOINT — Week [#] of 12

Stage: [0 / 1 / 2 / 3]
Date: [DATE]

TARGETS THIS WEEK:
1. [Target] — [Hit / Missed / In Progress]
2. [Target] — [Hit / Missed / In Progress]
3. [Target] — [Hit / Missed / In Progress]

KEY NUMBERS:
- Conversations this week: [#]
- New customers/signups: [#]
- Revenue this week: $[#]
- Pipeline (active leads): [#]

WHAT WORKED:
- [1 thing]

WHAT DIDN'T:
- [1 thing]

BIGGEST BLOCKER:
- [1 thing — and what you'll do about it]

NEXT WEEK'S TOP 3:
1. [Action + deliverable]
2. [Action + deliverable]
3. [Action + deliverable]

GUT CHECK (1-10):
- Confidence in the idea: [#]
- Energy level: [#]
- Pace (am I moving fast enough?): [#]
```

**If your confidence drops below 5 for two weeks in a row, stop sprinting and revisit your assumptions.**
